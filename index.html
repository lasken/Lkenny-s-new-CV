My Design & Dev Process

Every project follows a rigorous, research-driven workflow, from blank canvas to live URL.

Research

User interviews, competitive analysis, and data gathering to understand the problem space deeply.

Identify

Define pain points, user needs, and business goals. Formulate a clear problem statement.

Design

Wireframes, prototypes, and polished UI in Figma, tested and iterated before a single line of code.

Develop

Pixel-perfect implementation in VS Code, HTML, CSS, JS, React. Responsive from day one.

Deploy

Live on Vercel or GitHub Pages with speed insights, analytics, and continuous improvement.

About Me

I don't just design.
I solve problems.

Hey 👋 I'm Lasisi Kenny (LKenny) , a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer based in Ibadan, Nigeria. I believe the best digital products are born at the intersection of empathy, economics, and engineering.

My journey is unconventional in the best way: an economics graduate who fell in love with design, taught himself to code, and discovered that understanding human behaviour and market dynamics makes you a significantly better product designer. I approach every project with the mindset of both a researcher and an engineer, asking why before touching Figma, and testing thoroughly before hitting deploy.

From designing the NESAAU administrative platform (as its Treasurer) to building e-commerce tools, personal finance apps for Nigerian students, and creative brand experiences for music artists, I work across the full product stack. I write clean, maintainable code, produce client-ready prototypes, and can take a product from a napkin sketch to a live Vercel deployment independently.

I thrive in fast-moving environments, communicate clearly across disciplines, and care deeply about the people who will ultimately use what I build. If you want someone who brings both the creative vision and the technical execution, let's talk.
