My Design & Dev Process

Every project follows a rigorous, research-driven workflow, from blank canvas to live URL.

Research

User interviews, competitive analysis, and data gathering to understand the problem space deeply.

Identify

Define pain points, user needs, and business goals. Formulate a clear problem statement.

Design

Wireframes, prototypes, and polished UI in Figma, tested and iterated before a single line of code.

Develop

Pixel-perfect implementation in VS Code, HTML, CSS, JS, React. Responsive from day one.

Deploy

Live on Vercel or GitHub Pages with speed insights, analytics, and continuous improvement.

About Me

I don't just design.
I solve problems.

Hey 👋 I'm Lasisi Kenny — a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer based in Ibadan, Nigeria. I believe the best digital products live at the intersection of empathy, economics, and engineering.

My path is unconventional in the best way. I'm an economics graduate who fell in love with design, taught himself to code, and discovered that understanding human behaviour and market dynamics makes you a significantly better product builder. I approach every project as both a researcher and an engineer, asking why before opening Figma, and testing thoroughly before hitting deploy.

I've designed and built across the full product stack, from the NESAAU administrative platform to e-commerce tools, personal finance apps for Nigerian students, internal team management systems, and creative brand experiences for music artists. I write clean, maintainable code, produce client-ready prototypes, and can take a product from a rough idea to a live Vercel deployment, independently.

I thrive in fast-moving environments, communicate clearly across disciplines, and care deeply about the people who will ultimately use what I build. If you need someone who brings both creative vision and technical execution to the table, let's talk.
